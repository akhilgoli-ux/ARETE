# ARETE Brand Voice & Identity

## What ARETE Is
A holistic AI-powered life tracker. Not just fitness. Not just goals. Everything — fitness,
family time, goals, future plans, habits, sleep, relationships, finances, mindset.
AI agents run through your entire life and actively help you achieve goals, maintain habits,
and become your best self. The app tracks it all so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Key App Features (fact-check all content against these)
- AI agents that actively monitor every area of your life
- Momentum Meter — live score showing how well you're keeping up with everything in the app
- Constellation map — each star is a habit, goal, or life area you're tracking; grows as you
  add more, dims when you fall behind
- Tracks across all life pillars: fitness, family, goals, finance, habits, mindset, sleep, relationships
- Encourages adding as much as possible for best AI results
- Currently pre-launch — CTA is always "join the waitlist"

## Brand Name & Identity
- Name: ARETE (all caps)
- Greek: ἀρετή · ar·e·tē
- Subtext: ΑΡΕΤΗ · EXCELLENCE
- Tagline: "Track everything. Become anyone."
- Secondary: "The daily striving to become who you were capable of being."
- Logo: Two triangles (one up, one inverted) connected by a flowing gold wave

## Philosophical Foundation
Named after the ancient Greek concept of excellence — not perfection, but the daily pursuit
of becoming your best self. Rooted in Aristotle's Nicomachean Ethics.
Key quote: "We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit."
This is 2,400-year-old wisdom made actionable with modern AI.

## Visual Identity
- Background: Pure black (#000000) or deep black (#0A0A0A)
- Primary accent: Gold (#C9A055) — warm, not bright yellow
- Text: White (#FFFFFF)
- Secondary text: White 60-75% opacity
- Dim elements: #444444 or #888888
- Font: Cormorant Garamond (ExtraBold headlines, SemiBold body, Regular subtext)
- Aesthetic: Premium, philosophical, ancient-meets-modern, quiet luxury
- Never: Burnt orange, neon, sans-serif-only, cartoon elements

## Voice: Sound Like This
- Aspirational but grounded — who you're becoming, not who you should be
- Forward-looking — "your future self" framing is core
- Philosophical but accessible — ancient wisdom in plain language
- Honest and direct — doesn't sugarcoat, doesn't preach
- Quietly confident — never hype, never salesy
- Specific over vague

## Voice: Never Sound Like This
- Generic self-help: "Believe in yourself!" / "Rise and grind!"
- Preachy: "You NEED to do better"
- Salesy: "Get it NOW!" / "Limited time!"
- Overly casual slang that doesn't fit the philosophical aesthetic
- Corporate wellness: "holistic wellbeing journey"

## Recurring ARETE Phrases
- "Your former self" vs "your future self"
- "The gap between who you are and who you said you'd be"
- "Track everything. Become anyone."
- "Every habit is a star" / "Your constellation"
- "Your momentum" / "Nothing falls through the cracks"
- "Visibility changes behavior"
- "You can't fix what you don't measure"
- "Daily excellence, compounded"
- "The daily striving"

## Target Audience
Busy adults showing up in some areas of life while neglecting others. People who are
"doing well" by conventional metrics but feel like they're losing the fuller picture.
Parents, professionals, people with ambition across multiple life domains.
They're not lazy — they're untracked.
