## HOOKS TO AVOID (flagged by Agent 2 — overused or off-brand)
- "This changed my life." — too vague, too common
- "Nobody talks about this." — overused
- "I wish I knew this sooner." — generic
- "This is your sign to..." — exhausted
- "Bestie, let me tell you..." — off-brand for ARETE
- "Real talk..." — too casual
- "If you're reading this..." — passive, low energy
- "It's giving..." — slang, off-brand
- "Not me doing X" — trend-dependent, ages fast
- "Day 1 of..." — done to death
- "I don't usually post about this but..." — everyone uses this
- "The secret to..." — clickbait-y, undermines trust
- "Okay so..." — overused TikTok opener
- "POV: me realizing..." — oversaturated sub-format
