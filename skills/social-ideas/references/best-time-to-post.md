## TikTok — Self Improvement / Life Tracker Niche

### Best days
1. Tuesday (highest saves in this niche)
2. Thursday
3. Sunday (strong for reflective/philosophical content)

### Best time windows (user's local time)
| Window | Performance |
|--------|-------------|
| 6am – 9am | Strong — catches morning scrollers commuting/waking up |
| 12pm – 2pm | Good — lunch break scroll |
| 7pm – 9pm | Best overall — highest completion rates in evening |
| 9pm – 11pm | Strong for emotional/vulnerability formats |

### Format-specific timing
- Trojan Horse / UGC Rant: 7pm–9pm (casual evening viewing)
- Quote Drop / Aesthetic Mood: 9pm–11pm (reflective evening mood)
- Did You Know / Countdown: 12pm–2pm (informational lunch content)
- Day in My Life: 6am–9am (morning routine viewers)

### What to avoid
- Friday 5pm–8pm (people are out, lowest completion rates)
- Saturday mornings (low engagement in this niche)
- Posting more than once in a 3-hour window (algorithm penalizes)

## Instagram — Self Improvement / Life Tracker Niche

### Best days
1. Monday (fresh week energy, goal-setting mindset)
2. Wednesday
3. Sunday (planning for the week ahead)

### Best time windows (user's local time)
| Window | Performance | Best format |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| 6am – 8am | Strong | Carousels, motivational captions |
| 11am – 1pm | Best for saves | Educational carousels |
| 5pm – 7pm | Best overall reach | All formats |
| 8pm – 10pm | High engagement | Vulnerability / story formats |

### Format-specific timing
- Carousel: 11am–1pm weekdays (people save during lunch, read later)
- Static Ad / Story: 7pm–9pm (highest Story view rates)
- Reels: 5pm–7pm (peak Reels discovery window)
- Quote Drop: 8pm–10pm (evening reflection mode)

### What to avoid
- Tuesday/Thursday afternoons (lowest engagement in this niche)
- Posting Reels and carousels on the same day (competes with itself)
- More than 1 feed post per day (algorithm caps organic reach)

## Cross-platform posting rule

Never post the same content on both platforms at the same time.
Stagger by at least 4 hours. Ideal gap: 24 hours.
Post TikTok first — if it performs well, the Instagram version benefits from existing
social proof (you can reference "this TikTok blew up" in the caption).

---

## Frequency recommendations

| Platform | Ideal cadence | Minimum |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| TikTok | 5-7x per week | 3x per week |
| Instagram feed | 3-4x per week | 2x per week |
| Instagram Stories | Daily | 4x per week |

TikTok rewards volume more than Instagram. On TikTok, consistency beats perfection.
On Instagram, quality and saves matter more than frequency.
