## PLATFORM POSTING WINDOWS

### TikTok — Self Improvement / Life Tracker Niche

Best days: Tuesday · Thursday · Sunday (reflective/philosophical content)

| Window | Performance | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| 6am–9am | Strong | Morning scrollers, commuters |
| 12pm–2pm | Good | Lunch break |
| 7pm–9pm | Best overall | Highest completion rates |
| 9pm–11pm | Strong | Emotional/vulnerability formats |

Format-specific:
- Trojan Horse / UGC Rant: 7pm–9pm
- Quote Drop / Aesthetic Mood: 9pm–11pm
- Did You Know / Countdown: 12pm–2pm
- Day in My Life: 6am–9am

Avoid: Friday 5pm–8pm · Saturday mornings · Posting twice within 3 hours

### Instagram — Self Improvement / Life Tracker Niche

Best days: Monday · Wednesday · Sunday

| Window | Performance | Best format |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| 6am–8am | Strong | Carousels, motivational captions |
| 11am–1pm | Best for saves | Educational carousels |
| 5pm–7pm | Best overall reach | All formats |
| 8pm–10pm | High engagement | Vulnerability / story formats |

Format-specific:
- Carousel: 11am–1pm weekdays
- Static Ad / Story: 7pm–9pm
- Reels: 5pm–7pm
- Quote Drop: 8pm–10pm

Avoid: Tuesday/Thursday afternoons · More than 1 feed post per day

### Cross-platform rule
Never post same content on both platforms simultaneously.
Stagger by minimum 4 hours, ideally 24 hours.
Post TikTok first — strong TikTok performance gives the Instagram version social proof.

### Frequency
| Platform | Ideal | Minimum |
|----------|-------|---------|
| TikTok | 5-7x/week | 3x/week |
| Instagram feed | 3-4x/week | 2x/week |
| Instagram Stories | Daily | 4x/week |

## SEASONAL CONTENT CALENDAR

  ARETE angle: Most powerful day of the year to post about tracking and consistency.

### FEBRUARY
