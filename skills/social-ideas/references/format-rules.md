# Format Rules & Engagement Mechanics

## Universal Rules (every format, no exceptions)
- Hook lands in first 3 seconds / first 14 words
- Every post needs at least one of: save trigger, comment bait, DM-worthy moment, swipe bait
- CTA is always "join the waitlist" / "link in bio" — never "buy now" or "download"
- ARETE brand voice on every word (see brand-voice.md)
- Slides: 5-7 default, 10 absolute max, never exceed 10

---

## FORMAT 1 — TROJAN HORSE
Platform: TikTok / Reels | Length: 30-45s | Style: Phone quality, casual, one person

Structure:
- 0-15s: Trending/relatable topic. NO product mention.
- 15-30s: Natural transition. Product casually name-dropped as part of daily life.
- 30-45s: Return to original topic. End without hard sell.

Rules: Product mention must feel accidental. No CTA at end. On-screen text highlights product name briefly only.

---

## FORMAT 2 — UGC RANT
Platform: TikTok / Reels | Length: 20-30s | Style: Phone selfie, natural lighting

Structure: Personal frustration → thing they found that helped → honest reaction, no bow.
Rules: No clean ending. Cuts off slightly abruptly. Sounds like talking to one friend.

---

## FORMAT 3 — STORY TIME
Platform: TikTok / Reels | Length: 45-60s

Structure (Hook → Build → Turn → Land):
- 0-5s: Drop into middle of story
- 5-30s: Context, tension rises
- 30-45s: Turning point — ARETE is the turning point, not the product
- 45-60s: What life looks like now. Quiet, not triumphant.

---

## FORMAT 4 — FOUNDER STORY
Platform: TikTok / Reels / Carousel | Length: 45-90s or 6-8 slides

Rules: Why was ARETE built? Personal, vulnerable. References to constellation/momentum meter feel like discoveries. Must not sound like a pitch deck.

---

## FORMAT 5 — DAY IN MY LIFE
Platform: TikTok Slideshow / Instagram Carousel | Slides: 6-9

Structure: Time + honest line → natural moments → app appears in 2+ slides without explanation → reflective final thought.
Rules: App shown, never announced. Momentum meter or constellation can appear as screenshots with no caption explaining them.

---

## FORMAT 6 — VOICEOVER B-ROLL
Platform: TikTok / Reels | Length: 20-40s | Style: No face, lifestyle footage

Rules: Write voiceover script AND b-roll description for each line. 1 sentence per 3-4 seconds. Tone: reflective, slightly poetic.

---

## FORMAT 7 — BEFORE / AFTER
Platform: TikTok / Reels | Length: 12-20s

Structure: 3-4 text lines on black (before) → hard cut → 3-4 text lines (after) → ARETE logo + tagline.
Rules: Transformation must be subtle and believable. Not "I lost 50lbs" — more "I stopped ignoring what mattered."

---

## FORMAT 8 — DID YOU KNOW
Platform: TikTok / Reels / Carousel | Length: 20-35s or 5-7 slides

Structure: Real surprising stat → why this is happening → ARETE as structural fix.
Rules: Stat must be accurate. Cite source if possible.

---

## FORMAT 9 — MYTH VS REALITY
Platform: TikTok Slideshow / Instagram Carousel | Slides: 5-7

Structure: Hook slide → Myth/Reality pairs (one per slide) → ARETE as the path to "reality" column.
Design rule: Split each content slide vertically. Left = myth (dim, strikethrough). Right = reality (bright, bold).

---

## FORMAT 10 — COUNTDOWN
Platform: TikTok Slideshow / Instagram Carousel | Slides: 6-8

Structure: "N things [claim]" → one item per slide numbered → CTA.
Engagement rule: Slide 2 must cut off mid-item — show "1." and start of text only, forcing swipe.

---

## FORMAT 11 — GREEN SCREEN REACTION
Platform: TikTok | Length: 20-40s

Rules: Background content must be real and verifiable. Reaction should be genuine. ARETE comes in as natural response to the stat, not a segue.

---

## FORMAT 12 — COMMENT REPLY
Platform: TikTok only | Length: 15-30s

Rules: Comment shown on screen (TikTok's native reply feature). Answer the question genuinely first. ARETE comes in as context, not as an answer.

---

## FORMAT 13 — POV
Platform: TikTok Slideshow / Instagram Carousel | Slides: 5-7

Structure: "POV: [scenario]" → unfold scenario one beat per slide → resolution + ARETE.

---

## FORMAT 14 — DUET BAIT
Platform: TikTok | Length: 15-25s

Rules: Must end on open question or challenge begging a response. Keep it short — the duet IS the content. "Tell me one area of your life you know you're falling behind in."

---

## FORMAT 15 — TRENDING AUDIO
Platform: TikTok Slideshow | Slides: 5-8 | Style: Text only, no talking

Rules: Each slide syncs to a specific beat or lyric moment. Text readable in 1-2 seconds. Audio suggestion must be a specific searchable TikTok sound name.

---

## FORMAT 16 — QUOTE DROP
Platform: TikTok / Reels / Story | Length: 10-15s

Structure: Philosophical quote fades in line by line → attribution in gold → ARETE tagline → logo.
Design rule: Only gold and white on pure black. No gradients. No images. No exceptions.

---

## FORMAT 17 — AESTHETIC MOOD
Platform: TikTok / Reels | Length: 10-20s

Rules: No words except ARETE logo at end. Black, gold, constellation, momentum meter visual. Music is the only storytelling. Never include a CTA — this is brand conditioning only.

---

## FORMAT 18 — CAROUSEL
Platform: Instagram only | Slides: 5-7 default, 10 max | Canvas: 1080x1350px

Engagement mechanics:
- Slide 1: Swipe bait — incomplete thought or bold claim cut off
- Slide 2: Commitment hook — earns the next swipe
- Second-to-last: Most shareable / DM-worthy moment
- Last slide: Save trigger CTA ("Save this." / "Save for when you need it.")
- High swipe-through rate → Instagram re-serves post 24-48hrs later to new audience

---

## FORMAT 19 — STATIC AD
Platform: Instagram Story (1080x1920px) / Feed (1080x1350px)

Engagement mechanics:
- Stories: always include one interactive element (poll, question box, emoji slider)
- Interactive Stories get 52% higher retention than static
- One headline. One subheadline. One CTA. Nothing else.
- Feel: premium and authentic — never corporate or over-designed

---

## FORMAT 20 — AUTO
Agent 1 selects format based on:
1. Topic emotional temperature (high emotion → story-time or ugc-rant)
2. Platform (carousel performs better on Instagram than TikTok)
3. Performance memory signals (preferred formats weighted higher)
4. Pillar balance (neglected pillar → format that fits it naturally)
5. Research agent's latest trending format data
