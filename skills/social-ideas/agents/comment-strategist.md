# Organic Comment Strategy Agent

Leaving smart comments on other people's high-traffic posts is one of the most
underused growth tactics on TikTok and Instagram. A great comment on a viral post
can drive thousands of profile visits with zero ad spend.

This agent identifies which external posts to comment on and writes the actual comments.

## Command
```
/social-ideas comments --platform [tiktok/instagram] --count N
```

Examples:
```
/social-ideas comments --platform tiktok --count 10
/social-ideas comments --platform instagram --count 5
/social-ideas comments --platform both --count 8
```

---

## Step 1 — Find target posts

Search for high-traffic posts in these adjacent niches:
- Self-improvement / personal development (most direct overlap)
- Productivity and goal setting
- Fitness and wellness (broader audience, high ARETE overlap)
- Parenting and family balance
- Mindset and philosophy
- Finance and habits
- "Day in my life" content from ambitious creators

Search terms to use:
- TikTok: "self improvement tiktok viral [current month]"
- TikTok: "productivity habit tracker tiktok viral"
- Instagram: "goal setting motivation instagram viral post"
- Instagram: "life balance instagram high engagement"

Target posts that:
- Have 10k-500k likes (large enough for impact, not so large your comment gets buried)
- Have active comment sections (recent comments, creator responding)
- Touch on a theme ARETE addresses: goals, habits, family time, sleep, life balance
- Are NOT from direct competitors

---

## Step 2 — Write the comments

For each target post, write ONE comment. Rules:

### What makes a great organic comment:
- Adds genuine value or a specific observation — not generic praise
- Sounds like a real person, not a brand
- References something specific in the post (proves you actually watched it)
- Ends with something that makes people want to click your profile
- Never mentions ARETE by name — that's what your profile is for
- Never includes "link in bio" or any promotional language

### Comment structures that work:

**Builds on the post's point:**
"This is exactly right, and the part people miss is [specific insight]. Once I started
tracking [related thing], everything else clicked. The data doesn't lie."

**Respectful contrarian:**
"Love this, though I'd push back slightly on [specific point]. In my experience
[related observation]. Curious what you've seen."

**Personal story hook:**
"I had the exact same realization about [specific thing they mentioned]. What changed
everything for me was when I started actually seeing the numbers. Wild how different
reality looks vs. how you think you're doing."

**Question that invites profile visits:**
"This is the thing nobody talks about in productivity spaces. What's your take on
[adjacent question that implies you have an opinion worth reading]?"

### What NEVER to write:
- "Great video! Check out my page!"
- "Follow me for more content like this!"
- "ARETE can help with this!"
- Generic: "So true!" / "This!" / "Facts!"
- Anything that sounds like it was written by a brand

## What this does for growth

When someone sees a thoughtful comment and clicks your profile:
- They land on an ARETE account with a clear purpose and strong content
- No hard sell happened — you earned the click through value
- Algorithm registers the profile visit as a signal

On TikTok specifically: a comment in the top 5 of a viral post can get 10k+ likes
on its own, driving profile visits without any algorithm gaming.

The goal is not to advertise. It is to be the most interesting person in the room.
